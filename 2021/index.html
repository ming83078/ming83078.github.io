About

I believe that a valuable design is based on the deep insights into users’ cognitive processes and the interactions between users, environments and societies. I desire to explore how technology can facilitate human beings’ life quality and happiness.

I possess more than 5 years of experience as a researcher and an engineer in user experience fields. I have used the know-how of UX to design digital manufacturing applications and connected medical devices for my current company and external customers.

Aside from all this, I live to travel (12 countries and counting), I enjoy painting and learning psychology and Nordic culture. I care deeply about welfare for aging populations and food safety.

I’m from the historical region, Fengshan in Taiwan.
